G-Wagon (BRABUS)

Here is the world best and the powerful car.In this car you can travel whereever you want like offroading, travelling and many more!

The Brabus G Wagon 900 Rocket is based on the Mercedes-AMG G 63 and features:

  • Engine: V8, 4.5 L
  • Power: 900 Hp
  • Torque: 1250 Nm
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.7 s
  • Top Speed: 280 km/h
  • Dimensions: Length/Width/Height: 4.616/2.040/1.439
  • Wheels: BRABUS Monoblock Z “PLATINUM EDITION” ten-spoke forged wheel with naked-carbon Aero-Discs
  • Braking system: Front brake discs with a diameter of 400 mm (15.7 in) and fixed calipers, Rear brake discs with a diameter of 370 mm (14.5 in) and floating calipers
  • Weight: A little over 2.1 tons
